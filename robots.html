Pepper is a semi-humanoid robot manufactured by SoftBank Robotics, designed with the ability to read emotions. It was introduced in a conference on 5 June 2014, and was showcased in SoftBank Mobile phone stores in Japan beginning the next day.

Vector is a curious, independent and powered by AI and state-of-the-art robotics. Vector is your sidekick who's up for anything but mostly just wants to make you laugh.

Asimo is a humanoid robot designed to be a helper to people. It can run, dance, hop, and kick a soccer ball. It travels the world as an ambassador to robokind, making humans excited about robotics.

TOPIO ("TOSY Ping Pong Playing Robot") is a table tennis playing robot developed by TOSY. It was first unveiled at the Tokyo International Robot Exhibition in 2007. Since then there has been two newer version made. The newest version (TOPIO 3.0) stands just over 6'2" and weighs 264 pounds. All the TOPIO robots use artificial intelligence to learn and improve while playing.
